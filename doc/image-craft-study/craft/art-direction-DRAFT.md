# Art direction — composition · light · colour · narrative · medium · the award bar

> The aesthetic-taste layer: how a *generative image* earns the word **award-winning** rather than
> merely *generated*. The sibling of [`visual-foundations.md`](../../../plugins/atelier/knowledge/canon/visual-foundations.md)
> (which judges the screen). Most "it looks AI / flat / generic" reactions are a named violation here —
> cite the name and the **corpus anchor** (an id from
> [`../corpus/exemplars.jsonl`](../corpus/exemplars.jsonl)) so the fix is teachable, not a vibe.
>
> **How to wield it:** an image reviewer is heavyweight because it is *grounded*. For every finding name
> **(a)** the principle, **(b)** the violation, **(c)** the corpus exemplar that does it right. A
> finding-free pass is *earned*, never assumed — and the [artifact floor](#7-the-artifact-floor) caps
> the score before any of this taste matters.

## 1. Composition — where the marks sit

> *The eye is led on purpose, or it wanders. Award-tier framing is inevitable; entry-level is centred-and-flat.*

| Principle | Essence — exemplary vs entry-level | Corpus anchor |
|---|---|---|
| **Rule of thirds** | Subject on a third-node, not dead-centre — tension over inertia. *Broken:* bullseye centring with no reason. | small figures on the third against vast sky — genre-08 (*Your Name*); swirl-eye on a third-node — landscape-11 |
| **Golden ratio / dynamic symmetry** | The figure traces a φ spiral / armature, not a grid cell. *Broken:* arbitrary placement that "just sits there". | S-curve body on the golden section — genre-17 (Leibovitz); `composition:golden-ratio`/`dynamic-symmetry` |
| **Leading lines** | Edges, roads, rivers, gestures route the eye to the focus. *Broken:* no path in; the eye has nowhere to go. | architecture funnels to the citadel — genre-11 (Dylan Cole); river S-curve through depth — landscape-08 (Levitan); 16 exemplars carry `leading-lines` |
| **Negative space / breathing room** | Emptiness *is* a compositional element — it gives scale and calm. *Broken:* every pixel crammed; no rest. | tiny figure dwarfed by void — scene-11 (BR2049, Deakins); reserved white — genre-10; 12 exemplars carry `negative-space` |
| **Framing (frame-within-frame)** | A doorway / window concentrates and means. *Broken:* a flat rectangle with no inner stage. | the closing-doorway frame — scene-20 (*The Searchers*); peloton through a broken window — action-09 |
| **Focal hierarchy & focal point** | *One* dominant subject reads first; the rest supports. *Broken:* no focal point — the #1 generic-AI tell. | warm sunlit ruins anchor against desaturated mass — genre-01; 8 exemplars carry `focal-hierarchy` |
| **Balance (symmetry vs asymmetry)** | Weight is deliberately distributed — formal axis *or* dynamic off-balance, never accidental. *Broken:* lopsided by neglect. | axial monolith — style-02 (Cassandre, *Normandie*); asymmetric off-centre negative space — style-05 (Hokusai) |
| **Figure-ground (the gestalt)** | Subject separates cleanly from ground by value/edge/colour. *Broken:* subject melts into background mush. | silhouette reads instantly against smoky ground — genre-20 (Frazetta); 8 exemplars carry `figure-ground` |
| **Depth via overlapping planes** | Stacked fore/mid/far planes build real space. *Broken:* a flat sticker with no recession. | continuous-take layered rubble — scene-14 (*Children of Men*); `overlapping-planes`/`layered-planes` ×8 |

## 2. Light & shadow — where the light falls

> *Light is the narrator. Award-tier light is motivated and structured; entry-level is flat, even, sourceless "AI daylight".*

| Principle | Essence — exemplary vs entry-level | Corpus anchor |
|---|---|---|
| **Three-point logic (key/fill/rim)** | A directional key sculpts form; fill controls shadow; rim separates from ground. *Broken:* flat frontal fill, no modelling. | soft key + gentle fill sculpts the figure — genre-17 (Leibovitz); rim/key on body panels — genre-12 |
| **Chiaroscuro & tenebrism** | Form emerges from near-black by a single raking beam — drama by value. *Broken:* grey, even, no shadow structure. | tenebrism, the named engine — **style-08 (Caravaggio)**; figures out of cathedral dark — **genre-03 (Donato)**; light through a doorway as key — genre-14 |
| **Golden / blue hour** | Warm low key + long shadows (or cool dusk) carries time-of-day and mood. *Broken:* noon flatness; HDR over-baking. | alpenglow warm-vs-cool split — landscape-01 (Bierstadt); magic-hour rim — genre-08; golden-hour viz — genre-13 |
| **Volumetric / god-rays** | Light made tangible through haze/smoke/rain. *Broken:* a glow sticker with no medium to scatter in. | smoke-shafted temple — scene-06 (*Apocalypse Now*, Storaro); rain doubles every source — scene-07 (*Blade Runner*) |
| **Motivated light sources** | Every source is *in-world* and explains itself. *Broken:* unmotivated rim/glow that no lamp could cast. | candlelight-only key — scene-08 (*Barry Lyndon*); neon practicals as the whole script — scene-02 |
| **Value structure & notan** | The image reads as a strong light/dark pattern *before* colour. *Broken:* muddy mid-tone soup, no value plan. | Zone-System tonal control — landscape-12 (Ansel Adams); solid-black value in pure ink — style-19 (Miura) |
| **Contre-jour / silhouette** | Backlight collapses the subject to a shape against light. *Broken:* a flat cut-out with no rim or atmosphere. | contre-jour silhouettes of monstrous form — genre-05 (Beksiński); tunnel-mouth silhouettes — scene-01 (Deakins) |

## 3. Colour (art direction, beyond WCAG)

> *WCAG asks "can it be read"; art-direction asks "does it sing". Award-tier colour is a chosen script; entry-level is garish or muddy default-render.*

| Principle | Essence — exemplary vs entry-level | Corpus anchor |
|---|---|---|
| **Harmony schemes** | A chosen relationship — complementary / analogous / triadic / split-comp. *Broken:* random rainbow, no relationship. | tiny saturated orange sun vs blue-grey (complementary) — style-06 (Monet); analogous warm field — genre-19 (Rothko); 10 carry `complementary` |
| **Temperature contrast (warm/cool)** | Warm light against cool shadow gives form and depth. *Broken:* one flat temperature everywhere. | amber key vs blue-black shadow — scene-06; warm peaks / cool foreground — landscape-01 |
| **Limited / dominant palette** | A disciplined few hues + one accent — restraint reads as intent. *Broken:* every hue at full saturation ("clown-vomit"). | earthy palette + one hot-red accent — genre-20 (Frazetta); 15 exemplars carry `limited-palette` |
| **Atmospheric perspective (hue + value recession)** | Distant planes desaturate, cool, and lose contrast — depth in colour. *Broken:* far hills as saturated as near ones. | ranks of bamboo paling into haze — scene-05; layered skyline recession — genre-01; 9 carry `atmospheric-perspective` |
| **Colour as emotion / mood** | Hue carries feeling with no representational help. *Broken:* colour as mere fill, no register. | colour-as-content field — genre-19 (Rothko); teal-green fairy-tale script — scene-10 (*Shape of Water*) |
| **The colour script** | A frame-wide, deliberate palette plan tying the whole image to one emotional key. *Broken:* incoherent local colour choices. | near-monochrome irradiated orange — scene-11 (BR2049); desaturated steel-blue war — scene-04 (*Saving Private Ryan*) |

## 4. Narrative & mood — what the image *says*

> *An award image is a held breath of a story. Entry-level is a competent object that means nothing.*

| Principle | Essence — exemplary vs entry-level | Corpus anchor |
|---|---|---|
| **Implied story** | The frame implies a before/after — a whole tale at its edges. *Broken:* a static subject, no stakes. | threshold doorway implies a life of exile — scene-20; concept serves the article's idea — genre-10 (Veve) |
| **The decisive moment** | Cartier-Bresson: the single instant where everything aligns. *Broken:* a limp in-between frame. | shadow appears to strike the ball — **action-01**; the pointing-gesture instant — style-08; 19 carry `timing:*` |
| **Gesture / line-of-action** | A figure's pose flows along one expressive arc. *Broken:* stiff, axis-less, lifeless posing. | climber's body rising through the vertical — action-04; dancer's limbs into negative space — action-07; 9 carry `line-of-action` |
| **Atmosphere** | Air, weather, haze, light-mood saturate the frame as a felt presence. *Broken:* sterile, airless render. | komorebi / breathing forest — style-12 (Oga, Ghibli); dust-motion cue — action-20 |
| **Emotional register** | The image commits to *one* feeling — dread, awe, tenderness, menace — and sustains it. *Broken:* tonally neutral, says nothing. | oppressive memento-mori — genre-05; reverent memory-soaked interior — scene-03 (*Tree of Life*) |

## 5. Style & medium fidelity — excellent *of its kind*

> *An image must be excellent **of its declared style/genre** — a true Art Deco, not a pastiche; a believable
> golden-hour, not HDR clown-vomit; clean line-art, not muddy. The corpus is organised by exactly these
> categories — **genres · styles · scenes · landscapes · diagrams · action** — and each is a fidelity yardstick.*

| Principle | Essence — exemplary vs entry-level | Corpus anchor |
|---|---|---|
| **Movement authenticity** | A named style obeys its *own* grammar — period palette, line-logic, surface. *Broken:* a generic-AI gloss wearing the style as a costume (pastiche). | true Art Deco geometric reduction — **style-02 (Cassandre)**; whiplash Art-Nouveau line — style-01 (Mucha); Swiss grid rigour — style-04 |
| **Medium truth** | The image honours its medium's physics — watercolour reserves white, oil shows impasto, ink builds value by hatch density. *Broken:* a flat digital fake of the medium. | reserved-white wash — style-17 (Potter); impasto in the lights — style-18 (Rembrandt); cross-hatch tonal build — genre-10 |
| **Genre believability** | The scene reads convincingly *as its genre* — a real golden-hour landscape, a lived-in tavern, a photoreal viz. *Broken:* the tell-tale over-rendered "AI sheen". | photographic-not-CG archviz — genre-13; lived-in crowded cantina — scene-17; convincing material response — style-13 (Pixar) |
| **Constraint as discipline** | Hard limits (low-poly facets, sprite grid, panel grid) are *the* aesthetic when owned, not fought. *Broken:* fighting the constraint into mush. | deliberate faceting — style-15 (Reynolds); pixel-grid sprite discipline — style-16 (*Octopath*); nine-panel grid — genre-09 (*Watchmen*) |

## 6. The award bar — award-tier vs competent-but-generated

> *The line our heroes' first images fell short of. Award-tier is publication/gallery-ready: intentional in
> every pixel, with a name for every choice. Competent-but-generated is the entry-level trap — polished
> surface, empty craft.*

**Award-tier** clears all of §1–§5 *and* survives §7: one clear focal point, motivated structured light, a
disciplined colour script, a felt mood, true to its style, zero artifacts. Every choice is *nameable*
(cf. genre-03, style-08, scene-06, action-01).

**The entry-level trap** — the named tells of competent-but-generated:

| Tell | What it is | The fix it lacks (§) |
|---|---|---|
| **No focal point** | the eye wanders; nothing reads first | focal hierarchy §1 |
| **Flat lighting** | even, frontal, sourceless "AI daylight" | three-point / motivated light §2 |
| **Muddy or garish colour** | mid-tone soup *or* every-hue-maxed clown-vomit | limited palette / colour script §3 |
| **Cliché composition** | dead-centre bullseye, no leading line, no negative space | composition §1 |
| **Baked text / anatomy artifacts** | gibberish glyphs, melted hands — see §7 | artifact floor §7 |
| **Generic "AI sheen"** | over-rendered plastic gloss; style worn as costume, not obeyed | medium truth / movement authenticity §5 |

## 7. The artifact floor — hard fails that cap any image

> *Below this line, polish is irrelevant. These align with the image rubric's hard caps in*
> [`../../../plugins/pressroom/skills/design-reviewer/references/image-aesthetic-canon.md`](../../../plugins/pressroom/skills/design-reviewer/references/image-aesthetic-canon.md)
> *(Artifact-freedom dimension). A single instance caps the score regardless of every other virtue.*

| Hard fail | What disqualifies | Rubric tie-in |
|---|---|---|
| **Mangled anatomy** | extra/missing limbs, fused faces, melted hands where figures are central | caps the cell **≤ 2** (anatomy hard cap) |
| **Gibberish text** | baked pseudo-glyphs where legible text/labels were invited | route away from this model; note explicitly |
| **Melted geometry** | duplicated/fused/dissolved forms, incoherent hard-surface | Artifact-freedom **0–1** |
| **Broken perspective** | impossible vanishing, warped planes, non-Euclidean architecture | Artifact-freedom **0–1** |

> Every exemplar in the corpus clears this floor absolutely — that is the *precondition* of being in it,
> before any of its named virtues earn it a place.
