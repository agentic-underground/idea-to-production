## Shared infrastructure
- **The plugin survey** — both PLANs read across `plugins/*/{agents,skills,commands}` and the `docs/`
  tree. PLAN_0016 produces the taxonomy map (the survey of repeated families); PLAN_0017's audience index
  reuses the same per-plugin walk. The survey discipline is established once in PLAN_0016 and reused.
- **The two invariants both PLANs must preserve** — the **self-contained-plugin** rule
  (`${CLAUDE_PLUGIN_ROOT}`-only resolution) and the **canonical-copy promise** (KAIZEN/check.sh mirrored
  byte-identical, CI-verified by `scripts/verify-prereqs.sh`). No reorg or doc move may break either; the
  root README/CLAUDE/KAIZEN canons must not move.

## Definition of done
- Both PLANs are `completed` (the engine rolls EPIC completion up from its PLANs).
- `.pipeline/verify` is GREEN for each landed PLAN; `scripts/verify-prereqs.sh` (checks A/N/O canonical
  parity, H name/version parity, I doc-link resolution, P roadmap conformance) stays GREEN.

## Execution contract (for the builder)
The engine builds one PLAN at a time in `## Plans` order, running each PLAN's `## Construction process`
(FOUNDRY scoped to the `(EPIC, PLAN)` pair). PLAN_0016 (map first) precedes PLAN_0017. A failed PLAN
leaves its row `engaged` and resumes next tick; the EPIC lands only when both PLANs are GREEN.
