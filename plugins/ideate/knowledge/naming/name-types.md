# Name types — the taxonomy of how a name means

The canon of name *constructs*, each a distinct **generation vein**. The old generator coined
invented words only; this is the full net. The charter's **name-type strategy** (see
[`../../skills/name-search/references/discovery-protocol.md`](../../skills/name-search/references/discovery-protocol.md))
selects which veins fire — generation runs **one agent per vein**, never one per name.

Two axes frame every name:
- **Approach** — how directly it means: **Descriptive → Suggestive → Abstract**.
- **Construct** — what it's made of: **Real-word · Compound · Coined**.

Trademark strength rises as you move toward Abstract/Coined (more distinctive = more protectable);
comprehension falls. The right cell depends on the charter's risk appetite and audience.

## The veins

| Vein | What it is | Examples | Trademark | Use when |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Descriptive** | says the function plainly | StubHub, The Container Store, Pandora *(no)* | weak (hard to own) | clarity > distinctiveness; crowded SEO is acceptable |
| **Suggestive / evocative** | hints at a quality without stating it | Amazon, Stripe, Vercel, Patagonia | medium–strong | the sweet spot for most products — meaning + ownability |
| **Arbitrary real-word** | a real word unrelated to the product | Apple, Blackberry, Oracle, Caddy | strong | want instant familiarity + strong mark; accept SEO fight |
| **Compound** | two real words joined | Salesforce, Firefox, GitHub, Facebook | medium | function clarity + a hook word |
| **Portmanteau / blend** | two morphemes fused at a shared sound | Pinterest, Instagram, Microsoft, Netflix, Groupon | strong | want coined-feel that still reads as meaning |
| **Coined / fanciful** | invented, no prior meaning | Kodak, Xerox, Häagen-Dazs, Sonos, Tessl | strongest | maximal ownership; willing to build meaning from zero |
| **Acronym / initialism** | letters of a phrase | IBM, AWS, BMW, TQXR | weak–medium | a long true name needs a short handle (letters mostly taken short) |
| **Backronym** | a word first, expansion fitted after | QUASGO ("Quality-Assured, Systems GO"), Vim ("Vi IMproved") | strong | want a word that *also* encodes the philosophy as initials |
| **Numeronym** | a number compresses letters | k8s, i18n, l10n, a11y, 3M | medium | dev in-group shorthand for a long term |
| **Metaphor** | a concrete image standing for the value | Nike (victory), Amazon (vastness), Sherpa | strong | one vivid image carries the whole positioning |
| **Mythological / literary allusion** | borrows a named figure's equity | Hermes, Apollo, Prometheus, Janus, Pandora | strong | gravitas / borrowed connotation (see [`dev-tool-conventions.md`](dev-tool-conventions.md)) |
| **Animal / nature** | an animal or natural form as archetype | Puma, Jaguar, Twitter (bird), Mailchimp | strong | an animal's traits *are* the brand personality |
| **Scientific-taxonomic** | a genus/species/constellation/element | Apus, Pavo, Grus (constellations); Otus (owl); Cygnus | strong | want a real-but-obscure word with built-in imagery |
| **Foreign-borrow** | a word from another language | Volvo ("I roll"), Audi, Samsung, Nivea | medium–strong | the meaning lives cleanly in another tongue (check connotation!) |
| **Real-word respell / disemvowel** | a word re-spelled for ownability | Lyft, Flickr, Tumblr, Fiverr, Dribbble | medium | want a real word's meaning but a free namespace |
| **Onomatopoeia** | the sound *is* the meaning | Zoom, Twitter (tweet) | strong | energy / immediacy; a sound the product evokes |
| **Reduplication** | a repeated/rhyming cadence | TikTok, Kit-Kat, Jujube, Booboo | strong | maximal memorability / playfulness |
| **Founder / eponymous** | a person's name | Tesla, Disney, Ferrari | medium | a person's reputation *is* the equity |
| **Geographic** | a place (real or evoked) | Cisco (San Francisco), Fuji, Patagonia | medium | place carries the story / heritage |

## Notes

- **Don't run all veins every time.** The discovery brief's name-type appetite (descriptive ↔
  suggestive ↔ abstract; real-word ↔ compound ↔ coined) selects 4–8 veins — this is the
  waste-elimination discipline. A bold/abstract brief leans coined + arbitrary + scientific-taxonomic;
  a trustworthy/clear brief leans suggestive + compound + metaphor.
- **Short constructs are mostly taken.** Acronyms/initialisms ≤5 chars and one-syllable real words are
  near-saturated on GitHub/npm — prefer longer backronyms or coined/portmanteau when availability +
  zero-neighbour are hard constraints. (Verified pattern; see the checker's neighbour pass.)
- **Real-word and foreign-borrow veins MUST go through the connotation screen** — they carry the
  highest cross-language-blunder and trademark-collision risk. Flag, never self-clear.
