# Gate-keeper intake checklist

Before doing any work at your station, confirm the incoming artifact satisfies the previous
station's OUT / your IN contract (see value-station-handoff SKILL.md). If not, reject at
intake with: (1) the missing input, (2) which station owns producing it, (3) return.

Never silently do the previous station's job. That hides defects and corrupts the ledger.
